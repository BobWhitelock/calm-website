Welcome to CALM, the Swindon Children's Cancer And Leukaemia Movement.

CALM is a registered charity, run by parents who have all experienced what it is like to have a child diagnosed with cancer.

CALM helps families that have a child receiving treatment at the Great Western Hospital in Swindon, Wiltshire.

Most of the families CALM supports are based in the Swindon and Marlborough area.

CALM is a purely voluntary charity with no paid employees.

We aim to keep all expenses as low as possible, so we can ensure money donated can help the families who need it.

CALM receives no funding from the NHS. All funds are raised by our families, with support from the local community.
